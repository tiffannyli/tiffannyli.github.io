St.Paul's Cathedral

Written on: March 6, 2020

Designed by Sir Christopher Wren and located in the City of London, St. Paul's Cathedral is one of the most famous and recognizable landmark in London.

I first visited St. Paul's Cathedral on July 2, 2019 when I toured around the outside of the Cathedral. It was pass visiting hours so my friend and I were not able to go in. It's actually very important to check opening times for the Cathedral, it closes at certain times for church service.


View of St.Paul's Cathedral inbetween two buildings



The outside of the Cathedral is already breathtaking, but the interior will not disappoint. St. Paul's Cathedral is one of those buildings that you really need to visit the actual site to really appreciate its beauty. I went back to visit several times during my study abroad in London.


Outside of St.Paul's Cathedral



Bonus: If you are able to walk up couple hundreds of narrow staircases, I strongly recommend taking that 528 steps to the Golden Gallery. At there, you will be able to have a bird's view eye of the City of London, including famomus landmarks along River Thames.
